About Us

"Ketchup Berries", you might be wondering why is a dessert store is named like that? Well, if you reviewed our menu, you will realize that our desserts are all related to strawberries. Strawberries and ketchup, how are the two related? The color red! Ketchup is red and the word 'strawberries' got the word 'berries' in it. So we thought it would be a good idea to name our store "Ketchup Berries". With our store named, a slogan was created: "Ketchup" Berries! Spread the sweetness! Which could also be interpreted as "Catch up Berries! Spread the sweetness!" We felt it would be nice to spread the sweetness of our desserts to everyone. The ones that might also carry happiness to you.
