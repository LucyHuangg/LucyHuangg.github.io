Visit Us

We're located beside The International School of Macao, filling in the place with the scent of fresh strawberries and cakes. You can find us on the right side of the street after arriving at the school.

If you're looking for a cozy place to relax and enjoy some delicious food, you've come to the right place! We are always here to share the sweetness mixed with strawberries, cakes, and our love for food with you.

If this is your first time visiting us, the classic strawberry cake could be a good choice. Strawberry desserts are always a good choice to enrich your day!

Whether this store is planned in your schedule or just passing by, you are always welcome to stop by and enjoy the sweetness with us!
